Welcome to Fantasy Five

Fantasy Five is a game that casts you in the role of a Fantasy manager of Fantasy Five players. You must pick a squad 5 players from the website and calculate total expenses and so on.Fantasy Five is done almost all online, so competitors can live in different places. Your team gets points for how your players perform in real life.
